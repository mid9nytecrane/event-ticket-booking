Frequently Asked Questions

Payments are processed and sent to your registered mobile money number within 24 hours after your event concludes. If we miss this deadline, you'll receive an additional 5% compensation automatically added to your payout.

We currently support MTN Mobile Money, Vodafone Cash, and AirtelTigo Money. You'll need to provide a valid mobile money number during registration that matches one of these providers.

No hidden fees. We charge a flat 5% platform fee on ticket sales. This covers payment processing, customer support, and platform maintenance. You keep 95% of all ticket revenue.

After registration, we'll send a small test transaction (less than ₵1) to your mobile money number. You'll need to confirm the exact amount received to verify your account. This ensures payments are sent to the correct number.
